Getting Started

We are a small family-run local business, operating in Marakson since 2007.

We get about 100 people playing at Diggers each week. Beach volleyball is a great family sport, so we think more young people would enjoy being part of what we offer, which is:

- fun
- social volleyball and competitive volleyball tournaments
- socialising
- licensed bar (happy hour every day from 6-7pm)
- available to hire for functions – fun and fitness


We get players of all ages, but mostly they are 35 – 50.
